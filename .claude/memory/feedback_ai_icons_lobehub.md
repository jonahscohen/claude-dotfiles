When looking for icons representing AI companies, models, or products (Claude, OpenAI, Gemini, Mistral, etc.), search Lobehub first: github.com/lobehub/lobe-icons

**Why:** Lobehub maintains a comprehensive, well-organized icon set for AI providers with consistent sizing, dark/light variants, and color/monochrome options. Better quality and coverage than searching individual brand pages.

**How to apply:** Before sourcing any AI provider icon, check `https://github.com/lobehub/lobe-icons/tree/master/packages/static-png/public/dark/` for SVG files. Use the exact path data from these files. This applies to any project, not just improv.
