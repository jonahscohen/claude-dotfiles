# PRODUCT.md

## Register

**Brand**

## Primary Users

Digital creative practitioners across roles - PMs, AMs, designers, engineers, and other technical-adjacent collaborators working in the digital creative space. Messaging and presentation should land for non-CLI-comfortable audiences while staying credible to engineers who actually use the tools.

## Brand Personality

Professional, technical, restrained, plainspoken. Voice of a developer who has shipped real tools, not a marketing copywriter. Specific over evocative. Confidence without bravado.

## Anti-References

What this should NOT look like:

- Generic SaaS marketing patterns
- Hero gradients with floating product mockups
- Screenshot carousels of fake dashboards
- "Request a demo" CTAs
- "We use AI to do X" subheadings
- Notion-clone minimalism
- AI-startup glossiness

## Strategic Principles

- Each tool gets equal billing - no hero tool, no de-emphasized tool
- Navigation reflects the toolkit structure
- Visual rhythm matches the toolkit architecture
- User can scan in 5 seconds and know what's available
