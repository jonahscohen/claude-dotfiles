# PRODUCT.md

## Register

**Brand**

## Primary Users

Independent creative directors, brand strategists, and studio principals evaluating who to hire for a six-figure brand engagement. They are sophisticated visual consumers and impatient with anything that looks like a template.

## Brand Personality

Editorial, confident, restrained. Voice of a senior designer who has shipped real brand systems for grown-up clients. Specific over evocative. Confidence without bravado.

## Anti-References

What this should NOT look like:

- Generic agency portfolio templates with hero gradients
- Carousel-of-logos walls
- "Award-winning" or "passionate about" marketing copy
- Hovering 3D mockups of phone screens
- Notion-clone minimalism
- AI startup glossiness

## Strategic Principles

- Typography is the primary brand expression
- Case studies lead with strategy, not pretty pictures
- Restraint is the differentiator
- One serif display face, one neutral body face, one mono for captions
