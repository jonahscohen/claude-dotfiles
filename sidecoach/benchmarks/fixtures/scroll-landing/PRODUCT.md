# PRODUCT.md

## Register

**Brand**

## Primary Users

Founders evaluating whether to adopt a developer-tooling product. They visit once on a desktop browser, scroll the landing page, and decide in under ninety seconds whether the product is worth trying.

## Brand Personality

Confident, kinetic, technical. The motion is the brand argument: scroll-driven reveals show that the team can ship animation that respects intent and reduced-motion preferences.

## Anti-References

- Parallax that makes the user feel motion sick
- Scroll-jacking that prevents normal scrolling
- Hero videos that autoplay with sound
- Animation that fires repeatedly on every scroll up and down
- Decorative motion with no narrative purpose

## Strategic Principles

- Every animation respects prefers-reduced-motion
- Scroll-driven means scrubbed, not triggered-once
- Lenis smooth scroll for the marketing page, native scroll inside any content surface
- Reveals at 30% of viewport, not 100%
- Exit animations 2-3x faster than entrance
