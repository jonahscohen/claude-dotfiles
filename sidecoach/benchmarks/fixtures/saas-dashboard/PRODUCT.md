# PRODUCT.md

## Register

**Product**

## Primary Users

Operations engineers, on-call SREs, and platform team leads who live in this dashboard for hours per day. They evaluate it on density, keyboard efficiency, and how well it stays out of the way during an incident.

## Brand Personality

Workmanlike, terse, instrument-panel. Voice of a tool the user trusts because it has never lied to them. No personality for personality's sake. Information first, chrome second.

## Anti-References

- Marketing-site design language pulled into the product (gradients, hero copy, "delight")
- Mystery icons without labels
- Animation as decoration
- Dashboards that scroll horizontally to hide density
- Empty states with cartoon illustrations

## Strategic Principles

- Keyboard shortcuts on every primary action
- Dense by default, with an explicit reading mode
- Status colors that work in colorblind palettes
- Tabular figures everywhere a number appears
- Reduced-motion is the default, not a toggle
